Dice Game Rules

You get three rolls per game. The player who scores the highest at the end wins!

If any of the two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round is 0.

  • ex: if you roll a 6 and 1, you get a score of 0

If a pair of the same numbers are rolled then the score is the total of the two dice multiplied by 2

  • ex: if you roll 5 and 5, you get a score of (5+5)*2 = 20

Any other combination of dice results in the total value of the two dice.

  • ex: if you roll a 3 and 2, you get a score of 3+2 =5
